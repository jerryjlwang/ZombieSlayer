Model Evaluation Metrics

Choosing the right evaluation metric depends on the task. Classification tasks use accuracy, precision, recall, and F1. Regression tasks use MSE, RMSE, and MAE. Generation tasks are harder to evaluate automatically; BLEU, ROUGE, and BERTScore are common proxies.
